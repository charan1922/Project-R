# Project-R (DeepQuant)

Project-R is a sovereign AI-driven algorithmic trading ecosystem that reverse-engineers and automates "Smart Money" execution patterns. It synthesizes verified P&L data with a proprietary 4-factor engine to build a deterministic AI agent that scans and executes like a professional market maker.

## The 4 R-Factors

The engine tracks four key market indicators to identify "Smart Money Footprints":

| Factor | What it measures |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Volume** | Total shares traded — participation gate |
| **Open Interest** | Buildup/decline of outstanding contracts — directional compass |
| **Bid-Ask Spread** | Urgency and liquidity consumption |
| **Turnover** | Total value of transactions — quality filter |
